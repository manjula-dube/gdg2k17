  • #DevFest17

    GDG DevFests are large, community-run developer events happening around the globe focused on community building and learning about Google’s technologies.

    DevFest events cover multiple product areas such as Android, Firebase, Google Cloud Platform, Google Assistant, Flutter, machine learning with TensorFlow, and Mobile Web.

    Dev Fest is an event celebrating the creativity of amazing developers and designers around us. It provides developers a stage to share their knowledge and learn from others. Startups looking for the right people, Open source authors showcasing their latest creations and App developers displaying their labors of love all under one roof! And what's more? It's free! So register now and join the festival !
